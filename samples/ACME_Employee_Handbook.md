## 1. Working Hours (근무 시간)
Standard working hours are 09:00–18:00, Monday to Friday, with a one-hour lunch
break. Flexible start times between 08:00 and 10:00 are permitted with manager approval.

## 2. Annual Leave (연차)
Full-time employees accrue 15 days of paid annual leave in their first year.
One additional day is granted for every two years of continuous service, up to a
maximum of 25 days. Unused leave may be carried over for up to one year.

## 3. Business Travel (출장)
Domestic travel expenses (transport, lodging, meals) are reimbursed against valid
receipts within company limits. International travel additionally covers a daily
allowance based on the destination region. Submit claims within 14 days of return.

## 4. Remote Work (재택근무)
Employees may work remotely up to two days per week with prior approval. Core
collaboration hours (10:00–16:00) require availability on chat and video.

## 5. Family Event Support (경조사)
The company provides congratulatory or condolence support for major family events
(marriage, birth, bereavement) according to the relationship and event type.
Contact HR to file a request with supporting documentation.

## 6. Education Support (학자금)
Employees with at least one year of service may apply for tuition assistance for
job-relevant courses, subject to prior approval and a minimum passing grade.
